Game of Crystals

Rules of the Game!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on any of the crystals, you will add a specific amount of points to your score.

You will win when you successfully match your score with that of the random number but you will lose if your score goes over the number (Think Price is Right!).

To make the game more interesting, the value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it and will change with each round.
